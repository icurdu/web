Iban Curdu


Freshly graduate graphic designer mastered in Branding and User Interface design. He has worked in the development of two startups: Haiku, an Android App that allows to create visual poetry by using text + photo with an genuine Polarioid photographic developing, and Meerkat a social network that shows messages arround you using geolocation. He completed his Internship developing the redesign of the full official website of the Germans Trias i Pujol Hospital of Badalona, one of the 20th best hospitals in Spain.

Fascinated by social media, interactive and collaborative environments, and supporter of education through transversal processes, Iban Curdu is the leader of Marketing&Communication Team and organizer in NAU3, project funded by EASD Pau Gargallo to foster multidisciplinarity and coworking, enabling spaces where heterogeneous creative groups are created. Currently, given his penchant for public speeching and enterpreneurship, Iban is the Graphic Media Creator of Inny, a non-profit organization project where youth entrepreneurs broadcast their experiences through conferences.
